About Ground Zero Lodging

Providing memorable cabin stays in the heart of Shawnee National Forest.

Our Story

Ground Zero Lodging was born from a passion for the outdoors and a love of the land. Years ago, we came to Southern Illinois on a hunting trip and were immediately captivated by the beauty of the Shawnee National Forest - the rolling hills, towering bluffs, and peaceful woods that stretch for miles. We fell in love with this place and knew we had to be a part of it. That connection to the land inspired us to create a place where others could come and experience the magic of the forest for themselves.

What started as a single family cabin has grown into a collection of thoughtfully designed retreats, each offering a unique way to enjoy the wonder of Shawnee National Forest. We believe that everyone deserves a place to unplug, recharge, and reconnect with nature. Our cabins are more than just places to stay - they're gateways to adventure, relaxation, and lasting memories. Whether you're watching the sunrise from your deck, hiking to Garden of the Gods, or simply enjoying the sounds of the forest from your porch, we want every moment to feel special.

As local owners and operators, we take pride in every detail - from the quality of our linens to the recommendations we share about the best hidden trails and local restaurants. We're not just renting cabins; we're sharing a piece of the place we call home.

What We Stand For

Our values guide everything we do, from cabin design to guest experience.

Guest-First Hospitality

Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priority. From the moment you book to the day you check out, we're here to ensure your stay exceeds expectations. We're always just a phone call or text away.

Respect for Nature

Shawnee National Forest is a treasure, and we're committed to preserving it. Our cabins are designed to harmonize with the environment, and we encourage responsible enjoyment of the forest.

Community Roots

We're proud to be part of the Southern Illinois community. We partner with local businesses, recommend local restaurants and attractions, and contribute to the region's tourism economy.

Quality & Comfort

We invest in our properties to ensure every cabin meets our high standards. Quality mattresses, clean linens, and modern amenities are the baseline, not the exception.

Authentic Experiences

We help our guests discover the real Shawnee Forest - from hidden waterfalls and local wineries to the best sunset viewpoints. Our insider tips help you experience the area like a local.

Continuous Improvement

We listen to guest feedback and are always improving. New amenities, upgraded furnishings, and enhanced services ensure that return guests always find something new to enjoy.
