About This Cabin

Sleeps 6 3 Bedrooms 2 Bathrooms

Welcome to Whispering Pines Lodge, our most popular cabin nestled among towering pine trees in the heart of Shawnee National Forest. This spacious three-bedroom log cabin combines rustic charm with modern comfort, making it the perfect base for your Southern Illinois adventure.

Step onto the wraparound deck and take in the sweeping forest views, or sink into the private hot tub as the evening sounds of nature surround you. Inside, the open-concept living area features a stone fireplace, comfortable seating, and large windows that frame the beauty of the forest.

The fully equipped kitchen has everything you need to prepare meals, from a full-size refrigerator to a coffee maker for those lazy mornings. Three cozy bedrooms with quality linens ensure everyone gets a great night's sleep, and the two full bathrooms mean no waiting in line.

Located just minutes from Garden of the Gods, Rim Rock Trail, and Pounds Hollow Lake, this cabin puts you at the doorstep of Shawnee National Forest's best attractions while providing a peaceful retreat to return to each evening.
