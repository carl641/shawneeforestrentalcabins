About This Cabin

Sleeps 4 3 Beds (Full + Twin Bunk Beds) 1 Bathroom

Welcome to #1 Quaint Hillside Cabin, a cozy retreat nestled on a peaceful hillside in the heart of Shawnee National Forest. This cabin combines rustic charm with modern comfort, making it the perfect base for your Southern Illinois adventure.

Step onto the deck and take in the sweeping forest views as the evening sounds of nature surround you. Inside, the open-concept living area features comfortable seating and large windows that frame the beauty of the forest.

The kitchenette has the essentials to prepare simple meals, including a coffee maker for those lazy mornings. Three cozy beds (one full and a twin bunk bed set) with quality linens comfortably sleep up to 4 guests, and a full bathroom rounds out the cabin.

Located just minutes from Garden of the Gods, Rim Rock Trail, and Pounds Hollow Lake, this cabin puts you at the doorstep of Shawnee National Forest's best attractions while providing a peaceful retreat to return to each evening.
